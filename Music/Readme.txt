Narrow Widget has following Features:
1. Two Views: One with Cover Pic and another Without Cover Pic. Tap on left side of the widget to switch between two views.
2. Tapping on middle part of the widget will take you to music app.
3. Music info is parsed using (a) Tasker Profile - uploaded here or (b) KWGT in built variables. You can choose which source you want in the Global Vars section of the widget.
4. Use the buttons on the right side to control playback
